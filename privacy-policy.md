# Privacy Policy for Love Booth

**Last updated:** 21 January 2026

Love Booth respects your privacy. This app is designed to work entirely on your device and does not require you to create an account or share personal information.

## 1. Information We Collect

Love Booth does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Specifically:

- No names, email addresses, or phone numbers are collected
- No photos are uploaded to our servers
- No location data is accessed
- No contacts or device identifiers are collected

All photo processing happens locally on your device.

## 2. Photos and Media Access

Love Booth requires access to your device’s photos or camera only to allow you to:

- Select images
- Capture photos
- Create photo strips

Your photos:

- Remain on your device
- Are never uploaded, stored, or shared by us
- Are discarded when you close or reset the app

## 3. Data Sharing

Love Booth does not share any data with third parties.

There are:

- No analytics tools
- No advertising SDKs
- No third-party trackers

## 4. Internet Usage

Love Booth does not require an internet connection for its core functionality.

If internet access is available, it may be used only to load the app interface when hosted as a web application. No personal data is transmitted.

## 5. Children’s Privacy

Love Booth does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected, the app is safe for general audiences.

## 6. Open Source

Love Booth is an open-source project. The source code is publicly available, allowing anyone to verify how data is handled.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app’s functionality changes in a way that affects privacy.
